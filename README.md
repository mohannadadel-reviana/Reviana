##  Project Overview
This project aims to build and scale a competitive perfume brand in the Egyptian market through a clear digital-first strategy, strong brand positioning, and data-driven marketing execution. The focus is on creating emotional connections with customers while delivering perceived luxury at an accessible price point.

---

## Project Objectives
- Build strong brand awareness and establish a clear market presence. 
- Create trust in the brand’s originality, authenticity, and product quality. 
- Achieve short-term sales growth through targeted performance campaigns.
- Develop long-term brand equity that supports sustainable growth and enables future regional expansion.  

---

##  Project Scope
- Market research and competitor analysis to define positioning and opportunities.  
- Buyer persona definition and customer journey mapping.  
- Brand messaging framework and Unique Selling Propositions (USPs).  
- Monthly content calendar and posting strategy for social media platforms.  
- Launch campaign planning including objectives, budget allocation, and KPIs. 
- Digital engagement and conversion optimization strategy. 
- Performance tracking, analysis, and optimization recommendations.

>  **Final Deliverable:** A clear, executable digital marketing roadmap that supports brand awareness, engagement, and sales growth.
